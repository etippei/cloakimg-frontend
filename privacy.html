🔒 Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters. We process 100% of images locally on your device.

📌 Key Promise: All image processing happens locally in your browser. Your photos are never uploaded to our servers, never stored, and never shared with third parties.

1. Data Collection

We collect minimal data to provide and improve our services:

  • Account Information: Email address and password (encrypted) for registered users.
  • Usage Analytics: Anonymous usage statistics (e.g., which tools are used, number of operations).
  • Payment Information: Processed securely by Gumroad. We never store credit card details.

2. Image Processing

Your images are 100% private:

  • All AI processing (upscaling, background removal, compression, conversion, resizing) runs locally in your browser or on our secure VPS.
  • Images are never stored on our servers after processing.
  • We do not use your images for training AI models.
  • No third-party services access your images.

3. Cookies & Tracking

We use essential cookies for authentication and preferences. We do not use third-party tracking cookies for advertising.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • Encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS).
  • Encrypted password storage (SHA-256).
  • Regular security audits.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your account data.
  • Opt out of marketing communications.
  • Request a copy of your data.
