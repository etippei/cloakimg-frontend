🔒 Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters. We process 100% of images locally on your device.

📌 Key Promise: All image processing happens locally in your browser. Your photos are never uploaded to our servers, never stored, and never shared with third parties.

1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal data to provide and improve our services:

  • Account Information: Email address and password (encrypted using SHA-256) for registered users.
  • Usage Analytics: Anonymous usage statistics (e.g., which tools are used, number of operations) to help us improve the service.
  • Payment Information: Processed securely by Gumroad. We never store or have access to your credit card details.

2. Image Processing & Storage

Your images are 100% private:

  • All AI processing (upscaling, background removal, compression, conversion, resizing) runs locally in your browser or on our secure VPS with automatic deletion.
  • Images are never stored on our servers after processing is complete.
  • We do not use your images for training AI models.
  • No third-party services access your images at any point.

3. Cookies & Tracking

We use essential cookies only for:

  • Authentication (keeping you logged in).
  • Preferences (remembering your settings).

We do not use third-party tracking cookies for advertising purposes. You can manage cookie preferences in your browser settings.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • Encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS).
  • Encrypted password storage (SHA-256 hashing).
  • Regular security audits and updates.
  • Limited access to user data.

5. Your Data Rights

Under GDPR and other privacy regulations, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data.
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your account and data.
  • Restriction: Limit how we use your data.
  • Data Portability: Receive your data in a structured format.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at etiplpl@outlook.com.

6. Data Retention

We retain your account data as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all associated data is permanently removed within 30 days.

7. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Cloudflare: For CDN, security, and D1 database hosting.
  • Resend: For sending transactional emails (verification, password reset).
  • Gumroad: For processing subscription payments.

These services are GDPR-compliant and process data in accordance with their respective privacy policies.

8. Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us immediately.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page. Changes are effective immediately upon posting.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Email: etiplpl@outlook.com
  • Response Time: Within 24 hours
